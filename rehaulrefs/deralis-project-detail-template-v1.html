What it is

Compliance for the French equipment rental industry.

TraviXO Systems is a multi-tenant compliance SaaS for the French equipment rental industry. It tracks the periodic regulatory inspections, the VGP, that every rental company in France is legally required to perform on the equipment they put in front of customers.

Missed inspections are a liability problem and a regulatory problem, and the way most rental companies track them today is through a mix of paper folders, individual spreadsheets, and the memory of whoever has been there longest.

The understanding behind it comes from time spent inside the equipment rental industry, close to how this work actually gets done. The product is shaped around those workflows rather than around a generic compliance template.

Built solo. Live. Running real inspection workflows in production.

What I built

The pieces that make the system work.

01

Multi-tenant foundation

Each rental company operates in its own isolated workspace, with strict separation of data between tenants.

02

Equipment register

Every piece of rentable equipment is tracked with its identification, category, regulatory class, and inspection schedule. The register is the single source of truth that every other part of the system depends on.

03

Inspection workflow

Inspections are scheduled automatically based on regulatory frequency, surfaced as work to do, and recorded with the inspector identity, date, findings, and any required follow-up. Nothing is recorded on paper and forgotten.

04

Audit trail and exports

Every action is logged in a way that survives an audit by the regulator. Inspection records can be exported as compliance reports without anyone having to assemble a folder by hand the night before.

05

Role-aware access

The branch manager, the technician, and the head office accountant see different views of the same data. Each role sees what is theirs to act on, and only that.
