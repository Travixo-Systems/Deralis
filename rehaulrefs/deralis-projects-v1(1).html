Case study

TraviXO Systems

B2B software · Equipment rental · VGP compliance · Live

TraviXO is compliance software built for the French equipment rental industry. The system gives teams one place to track equipment, manage compliance, and see what needs attention next.

Time inside the equipment rental industry at Loxam put me close to how these operations actually run. The deeper picture of how VGP compliance fails came from studying the regulation and from conversations with rental teams.

Over time, the same failure points kept surfacing. Inspections get missed. Certificates end up scattered across emails or folders. The audit trail depends on someone remembering to keep it updated. The system was built around removing those failure points.

Built solo. Live. Designed as a working operational system, not a concept.

Government Application Portal
